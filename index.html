Get Started

Steps to Access Your BlockFi Account

1. Navigate to the BlockFi Website

Visit BlockFi’s official website to begin the login process. Always ensure you’re using the correct URL to avoid phishing scams.

2. Locate the Login Button

Click the “Log In” button in the top-right corner of the homepage.

3. Enter Your Login Credentials

  • Email Address: Input the email associated with your BlockFi account.
  • Password: Use your secure password. Ensure it includes a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters like @, #, $.

4. Complete CAPTCHA Verification

Complete the CAPTCHA challenge to confirm you are a legitimate user and not an automated bot.

5. Authenticate with 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

If you’ve enabled two-factor authentication, input the verification code sent to your registered device or generated by your authenticator app.

6. Access Your Dashboard

Once authenticated, you’ll be redirected to your account dashboard. From there, you can monitor balances, review transactions, and manage investments.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Forgot Your Password?

  • Click the “Forgot Password” link on the login page.
  • Enter your registered email address.
  • Follow the instructions in the password reset email to create a new password.

Locked Out of Your Account?

If your account is locked due to multiple unsuccessful login attempts, contact BlockFi support via support@blockfi.com to regain access.

Lost 2FA Access?

If you lose access to your 2FA device, use backup codes provided during the 2FA setup. Alternatively, reach out to BlockFi’s support team for assistance.
